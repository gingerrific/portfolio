My final project during my time at The Iron Yard was an incredible collaboration to create a leader board for the iOS game Hunger Crunch, created by a local South Carolina charity, Rice Bowls. Using Parse, we created a leader board backend that allowed people to create their own unique contests and tournaments directly on the web. The webapp generated unique tournament codes, allowing each user to compete against their real-life peers in order to battle world hunger.
